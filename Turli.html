Turli

By Eva
Cook Time Calories Serves
20 1000 7 People

Recipe Key

Vegan
Dairy Free

Ingredients

4 tablespoons olive oil
3 medium onions
2 carrots
3-4 peppers
3-4 eggplants
2 small zucchinis
3-4 potatoes
250 grams okra
4-5 tomatoes
3-4 garlic cloves
A handful of parsley
1 teaspoon Vegeta seasoning*
Water
1 tablespoon Himalayan salt

* Vegeta is a popular ingredient in many Eastern European recipes.

  1. Cut every vegetable in small pieces.

  2. Pour the olive oil in a large frying pan and add the onions. Cook them for 2-3 minutes or until golden and then add the peppers, the eggplants, the zucchinis, the potatoes, the okra, and the carrots.

  3. Stir the vegetables for 2 minutes and add then add the tomatoes, the parsley, the chopped garlic cloves and the Vegeta seasoning.

  4. Poor enough water so as to cover all the ingredients and let it boil. Keep adding water to keep the vegetables submerged until boiled.

  5. Towards the end, add the salt and stir.

  6. Serve hot.
