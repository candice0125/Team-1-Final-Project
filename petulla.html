Petulla

By Eva
Cook Time Calories Serves
15 minutes 275 kcal 7 People

Recipe Key

Vegan
Dairy Free

Ingredients

1.5 kg all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons salt
1 tablespoon baker's yeast
115mL lukewarm water
1 teaspoon sugar
Cold water to help with the moulding
Vegetable oil for frying

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour and the salt

  2. In a smaller bowl, mix the lukewarm water, the yeast, and the sugar.

  3. Add the contents of the smaller bowl into the larger one and use a wooden spoon to mix the ingredients and make the dough.

  4. Cover the bowl with a wet towel (or plastic wrap) and let the dough rise for 2-3 hours in a warm place. Once the dough has risen, put vegetable oil in a frying pan at high heat.

  5. In the meantime, split the dough into small parts. Wet your fingertips with the cold water, take each small part and flatten it with your hands giving it a circular shape (This is kind of like playing with playdough)..

  6. As soon as the oil in the frying pan is warm, lower the heat and place each stretched dough circle into the frying pan. Wait until the dough circles turns crispy and flip them so both sides get fried.

  7. You can enjoy petulla with jam, chocolate spread, hummus, etc.
