Pasta Napoletana

By Eva
Cook Time Calories Serves
30 minutes 350 kcal 5 People

Recipe Key

Vegan
Can be made vegetarian.

Ingredients

4 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
1 finely chopped red onion
3 chopped cloves of garlic
1 kg chopped cherry tomatoes
A handful of parsley
454g Barilla Linguine
Pinch of salt
Ground black pepper and basil to serve (optional)

  1. In a frying pan with olive oil, add the chopped onion and the cloves of garlic and fry over medium heat until golden

  2. Add the cherry tomatoes, the parsley, and a pinch of salt and cook gently for 15 minutes. Once cooked, set aside

  3. If you want al dente pasta, boil the linguine for 9 minutes in boiling salted water; if not, boil for 10-11 minutes. Do not drain the pasta water â€“ you might need it for later.

  4. Once the pasta is boiled, toss the linguine in the sauce and stir for about a minute or two. If you want a creamier sauce, add a little of the pasta water to the frying pan. As a dear Italian friend of mine has told me: ALWAYS toss the pasta into the sauce and not the sauce into the pasta!

  5. Serve warm with basil and black pepper to taste.
